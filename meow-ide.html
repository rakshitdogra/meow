📐 Grammar (EBNF)

program ::= statement*
statement ::= declaration | assignment | meow_stmt | if_stmt | while_stmt | for_stmt | func_decl | return_stmt | break_stmt | continue_stmt | expr_stmt
declaration ::= "purr" IDENT "=" expr
if_stmt ::= "hiss" "(" expr ")" block ("else" (if_stmt | block))?
for_stmt ::= "pounce" "(" IDENT "from" expr "to" expr ")" block
func_decl ::= "claw" IDENT "(" params ")" block
array_lit ::= "litter" "[" (expr ("," expr)*)? "]"
🐾 Fun fact: The word MEOW is the center of this language — it powers all output. Every meow call is a cat speaking to the world!
